> To that end, I've committed "Bio-Based Chat Room Activity Filtering" to

> constructed some time ago and and discarded because it didn't seem all
> that effective (I was trying to make something that handled both chat

Please compare with the fork.  You are dividing up a filter for rooms
and a filter for IMs, but you were the one that wanted to keep things
consistent.  If you are going to put a bot filter in SVN, just merge in
the fork.  Otherwise the lack of integration and rather limited

I've not had a single bot IM me or spam a channel .. or rather .. not
that I could see, and I have the challenge/response set to only

majority.  In fact, I'm pretty close to 100%.
